Reflection on Process

Reflection on the process is once again a multi-faced task. Especially two means can be distinguished. On one hand, process reflection can be part of the formative assessment, creating insight into participants’ competences and personal development. On the other hand, and more central for this part of the TRAIL, reflection is essential to open up the way towards future improvements.

The two dimensions intersect during reflections for personal future improvement. This type of reflection is essential to fully benefit from the ‘transformative’ potential of transdisciplinary projects. These transformations change the way stakeholders relate to the wicked problem at hand and are therefore regarded as a way of working on the problem at hand in itself.

How to Reflect for Future Improvements

Here, boundary crossing mechanisms can be utilized.

Tools: reflective diaries, fishbowl activity, Inner development goals.
