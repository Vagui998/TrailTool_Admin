Teachers role 

Teachers play an integral role in educating future generations. The instructional goals they set for themselves, the educational goals they set forth for students, the teaching methods they use and the way they assess students play major roles in the development of the student. 

We may have to move beyond the current higher education structures and attempt to innovate the pedagogical approaches for future generations.
