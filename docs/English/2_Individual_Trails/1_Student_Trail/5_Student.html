Student role

Without you, there would not be transdisciplinary education. The education part is for you. Therefore, you are a very important member of the team. This is an opportunity to work with professionals from academia and outside of it on an equal level. Through collaboration, you can find out the possibilities and difficulties of working in the real world.  

Additionally, this is a moment to share your ideas and let them listen to you. You are the professional of tomorrow, the new generation that can be a changemaker. So, use this time to develop your thinking, skills and find your intrinsic motivation and change the system!
