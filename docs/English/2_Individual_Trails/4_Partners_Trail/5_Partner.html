Partners role 

In transdisciplinary education, your role makes the difference, since you are representing society. The idea of transdisciplinary education is to bring education and science closer to society. In a collaboration with multiple partners, solutions to complex problems can be found.  

There are multiple possibilities of different partners following the quintuple Helix Model saying that we need collaboration between education, politics, society, economy, and the natural environment, to assist socio-ecological transition (Carayannis and Campbell, 2019).

  • Business/industry includes companies from different sizes.
  • Society including civilians, communities and ngos 
  • Governmental organization such as the municipality 
  • Environment, this takes the perspective of nature into account
