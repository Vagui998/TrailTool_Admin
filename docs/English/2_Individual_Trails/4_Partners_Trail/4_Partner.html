Embark on an Expedition into Transdisciplinary Collaboration

Introducing the TRAIL: a robust tool designed to shed light on the stages of transdisciplinary collaboration. The tool draws on insights from extensive scientific research and practical experiences of experts, students, policymakers, educators, and industry partners. Within the TRAIL, well immerse ourselves in the diverse phases of creating, participating in, and evaluating transdisciplinary projects.

Begin your expedition at the BASE CAMP, where youll assemble with your expedition team and acquire essential knowledge on fostering successful collaborations across diverse backgrounds.


Advance to the next phase: DESIGNING the transdisciplinary education. Delve into the complexity of co-creating projects and cultivating learning environments, for everyone involved. Arrive at the DESIGN RESEARCH STATION, also known as LOWER CAMP, where youll reflect on your journey and contribute to the further refinement of the TRAIL TOOL. Extend invitations to others interested in joining your expedition team, enriching the collaborative experience.
