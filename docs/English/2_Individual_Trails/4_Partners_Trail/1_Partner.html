Your Expedition Begins!

Welcome to the TRAIL, a practical roadmap crafted to guide you through the landscape of Transdisciplinary Learning. Emerging from the waters of real-world challenges, you bring a wealth of experience and practical know-how to innovative collaboration. Whether you seek a different perspective from the young generation of students, wish to update your theoretical knowledge, or explore how your business can have a more positive environmental and societal impact, transdisciplinarity can provide you with a new, future-proof way of thinking.

The aim of this TRAIL-tool is to infuse enthusiasm, equity, and real-world applicability into projects and especially education. Transdisciplinary learning provides the pathway to bridge the gap between schools and practice, between theoretical knowledge and daily life experience, and helps you transform in a fast-changing world.

Embarking on the TRAIL, whether you are experienced in transdisciplinary learning or new to that realm, promises an expedition filled with discoveries, food for thought, and practical gateways. You are strongly encouraged to collaborate beyond your comfort zones, to address serious challenges, and uncover opportunities for you to make a meaningful contribution.

In the initial phase of the TRAIL Expedition, immerse yourself briefly in the realm of Transdisciplinary Learning, gearing up to meet your expedition team at the Base Camp. Get ready for an expedition with fresh insights and learning opportunities. Welcome on track.
