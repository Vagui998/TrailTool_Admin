Joining Forces as a Researcher

Enter the TRAIL, a tool designed to illuminate the key stages of transdisciplinary collaboration, supported by extensive scientific research and practical insights from experts, students, researchers, educators, and societal partners. In the TRAIL, we explore variable aspects and phases of creating, participating in, and evaluating transdisciplinary education. You will also be able to participate in the research to further improve the general knowledge on the theme of transdisciplinary education.

Begin your expedition at the BASE CAMP, where youll assemble with your expedition team and acquire essential knowledge on fostering successful collaborations across diverse backgrounds.


Advance to the next phase: DESIGNING the transdisciplinary education. Delve into the intricacies of co-creating projects and cultivating conducive learning environments and development for all involved.
