Researchers role 

Transdisciplinary education is a derivative of transdisciplinary research, in which scholars from different disciplines are working together with non-academic partners on a wicked problem (OECD, 2020). Therefore, research is an important factor in transdisciplinary education and your role is to guide the project with research driven approach.  

The main question is how all different perspectives, both from education and non-academic can come together in the research that deals with a complex wicked problem.
